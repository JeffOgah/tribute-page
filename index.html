Ire Aderinokun

Ire Aderinokun at the WeAreDevelopers Conference, 2017

Ire Aderinokun is a self-taught UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer currently working remotely as a software developer at eyeo. With degrees in Experimental Psychology and Law, Ire is adept at technical writing and breaking down complex concepts to be easily understood at all levels. Since starting her blog, bitsofcode, in 2015, she has made numerous articles on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. She has now contributed over 150 articles and video tutorials to the development community. Ire is a huge advocate for open source and sharing within the development community. She contributes to open source projects and speaks at events to share her knowledge. She also ran a scholarship program to sponsor a few Nigerian women to complete a Udacity Nanodegree of their choosing for 6 months. 4 out of 7 candidates successfully completed the course. Some of her works are listed below.

    Products & Tools

  • The CanIUse Embed: An embedabble widget to display up-to-date data from caniuse.com. Displays feature support data for multiple versions of the 10 major browsers. Configurable, reliable, and fully responsive.
  • Opera Mini Tips: A collection of front-end development features not supported by Opera Mini and some crowdsourced workarounds for them.
  • Formhack: Formhack is a "hackable sass-based form reset". Created to be easily customizable for any site without users worrying about cross-browser compatibility and differences.
  • Gulp Email Workflow: A workflow for building HTML emails using Gulp. It comes with a default MailChimp-supported template.
  • Designer News for Slack: A Slack bot that fetches and displays stories from Designer News directly into Slack.
  • Open Writer: A free theme for the Ghost blogging platform. It has been consistently listed as one of the top free themes on their theme Marketplace, and has been featured in a list of "Superb Free and Premium Ghost Themes".
  • Khaled Bot: Khaled Bot is a fun bot for Slack that mimicks DJ Khaled. It was created by Ire as an excercise in learning basic AI and getting better at Node.js. It was featured on Product Hunt and TechCabal.
  • botsofcode: A Twitter bot for Ire's blog, bitsofco.de.
  • Messenger Hunt: Delivers the best new products from Product Hunt to user's Facebook Messenger.
  • Feature Queries Manager: an extension to developer tools to help users better work and test user feature queries.
  • app.bitsofco.de: A Progressive Web Application for her blog, bitsofco.de

    Startups

  • Bitkoin africa: Bitkoin Africa is Person-to-Person (P2P) cryptocurrency trading platform where users can sell and buy cryptocurrency from other users. They're based in Lagos, Nigeria and cater to Nigerians in Nigeria who are interested in trading cryptocurrency.
  • BuyCoins: BuyCoins (Y Combinator S18) is a cryptocurrency exchange for Africa. BuyCoins is the only exchange in Nigeria that allows Nigerians to buy and sell cryptocurrencies, ranging from Bitcoin and Ethereum, to Litecoin and Bitcoin Cash, directly with their local bank account or debit card.
